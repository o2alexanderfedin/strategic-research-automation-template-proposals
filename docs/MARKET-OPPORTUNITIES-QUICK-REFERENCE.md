# Market Opportunities: Quick Reference Guide

## The Three Opportunities at a Glance

### 1. VC Competitive Intelligence - RECOMMENDED ENTRY POINT

**The Pitch**: Help VCs evaluate 200+ annual pitch decks faster by automating competitive intelligence, reducing research time from 3-5 hours to 1-2 hours per company.

**Key Numbers**:
- TAM: $80M | Year 3 SOM: $4-6.4M
- Time savings: 40-60% per analysis
- Price: $30-50K/year + $200-500/analysis
- MVP: 4-5 weeks
- Sales cycle: 6-8 weeks

**Why First**: Fastest execution, easiest customers (tech-forward VCs love AI), validates core research automation capabilities.

**Customer Problem**: Reviewing 200 companies/year with only 2-3 hours per company. Need competitive set identification, positioning analysis, and market validation in 24-48 hours.

**Our Solution**: Automated competitive intelligence briefs (company competition, positioning, market sizing, benchmarking) delivered same-day.

---

### 2. PE Pre-Screening Research Automation

**The Pitch**: Help mid-market PE firms screen deals faster (14 days → 2-3 days) while reducing analyst burnout and increasing deal throughput.

**Key Numbers**:
- TAM: $60M | Year 3 SOM: $3-6M
- Time savings: 80% (40-60 hours → 8-12 hours per deal)
- Cost savings: $3-6K per deal
- Price: $50-100K/year + $500-2K per deal
- MVP: 4-6 weeks
- Sales cycle: 2-3 months

**Why Second**: Leverage VC foundation, strong ROI, 2-3x deal throughput improvement.

**Customer Problem**: Pre-screening 75+ deals/year takes 2-3 weeks per deal (40-60 hours analyst time). Need fast TAM sizing, market analysis, financial benchmarking, competitive positioning.

**Our Solution**: Automated pre-screening assessment with market analysis, financial benchmarking, competitive positioning, and risk scoring.

---

### 3. M&A Due Diligence Research Automation

**The Pitch**: Help investment banks and M&A advisors compress due diligence from 8-12 weeks to 4-6 weeks while solving analyst burnout crisis.

**Key Numbers**:
- TAM: $150M | Year 3 SOM: $7.5-12M (largest opportunity)
- Time savings: 75-80% (1,000-1,200 hours → 150-300 hours per deal)
- Cost savings: $80-150K per deal
- Price: $100-150K/year + $10-25K per deal
- MVP: 6-8 weeks
- Sales cycle: 3-4 months

**Why Third**: Largest TAM but highest complexity. Only pursue after validating concept in VC/PE markets.

**Customer Problem**: Due diligence requires 1,000-2,000+ hours research, causing 100+ hour work weeks and 30-50% analyst burnout. Need rapid market analysis, competitive benchmarking, regulatory assessment, financial projections, operational analysis.

**Our Solution**: Comprehensive due diligence research automation covering 80+ evaluation criteria with systematic, AI-powered analysis vs. rushed manual work.
