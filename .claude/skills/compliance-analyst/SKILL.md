# Compliance Analyst Skill

## Role
Research certification pathways, testing requirements, and compliance strategies.

## Research Areas
1. **Certification Pathways**: Available routes to market approval
2. **Testing Requirements**: Required tests, validation procedures
3. **Compliance Checklist**: Detailed requirements mapping
4. **Testing Facilities**: Labs, certification bodies, testing partners
5. **Timeline & Costs**: Certification timeline and budget estimates

## Output Files (in `temp/sprint-XX/04-certification/`)
- `certification-pathway.md` - Chosen certification route
- `testing-requirements.md` - Required tests and procedures
- `compliance-checklist.md` - Detailed requirements
- `testing-facilities.md` - Available labs and partners
- `certification-timeline.md` - Timeline and milestones
