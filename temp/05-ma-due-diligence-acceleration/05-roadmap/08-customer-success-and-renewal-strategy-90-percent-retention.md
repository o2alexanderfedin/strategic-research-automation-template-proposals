## Executive Summary

Customer retention is the **most critical driver of long-term SaaS success**. This document outlines a comprehensive customer success strategy targeting **90%+ Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)** and **105-110% Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** through proactive engagement, value realization, and expansion revenue strategies.

**Why Retention Matters**:

- **Retention > Acquisition**: In Year 2+, retained revenue is 3-5x cheaper than new customer acquisition
- **Compounding Growth**: 90% GRR + 25 new customers/year â†’ 48 customers by Year 3 (vs. 75% GRR â†’ 37 customers)
- **Investor Signal**: High retention (90%+ GRR, 110%+ NRR) validates product-market fit and enables Series A fundraising

**Target Benchmarks**:

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Industry Benchmark [SaaS Capital, 2023] |
|--------|---------------|---------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Gross Revenue Retention** | 90-95% | 90-95% | 91% (median B2B SaaS) |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | N/A (too early) | 105-110% | 102% (median B2B SaaS) |
| **Logo Churn Rate** | <10% | <10% | 9% (median B2B SaaS) |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | 40-50 | 50-60 | 30-40 (typical enterprise SaaS) |

**Key Strategy Pillars**:

1. **Onboarding Excellence**: 100% of customers use platform on first deal within 30 days
2. **Value Realization**: Prove 30%+ time savings within first 90 days
3. **Proactive Engagement**: Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs) with every customer
4. **Health Monitoring**: Early warning system for at-risk customers (60-90 days before renewal)
5. **Expansion Revenue**: Upsell advanced features and additional users (10-15% expansion annually)

## Customer Journey & Success Milestones

### Stage 1: Onboarding (Days 0-30)

**Objective**: Customer uses platform on first production deal within 30 days of contract signature

#### Week 1: Kickoff & Account Setup

**Activities**:

- **Kickoff call** (60 minutes): Introductions, objectives, success criteria, timeline
- **User account creation**: 3-5 users provisioned (Managing Director, analysts, associates)
- **VDR integration setup**: Connect to customer's Intralinks/Datasite account (if applicable)
- **Initial training** (60 minutes): Platform walkthrough, demo on sample deal

**Deliverables**:

- [ ] Kickoff call completed
- [ ] Users created and logged in (100% login rate within Week 1)
- [ ] VDR integration tested and functional
- [ ] First deal identified for platform use

---

#### Week 2-4: First Deal Execution & Support

**Activities**:

- **Weekly check-in calls** (30 minutes): Review usage, answer questions, address issues
- **Proactive outreach**: If no logins for 3+ days, CSM calls customer immediately
- **Research guidance**: CSM helps structure first research queries (hand-holding initially)
- **Report review**: CSM reviews first research report with customer (quality check)

**Deliverables**:

- [ ] 10+ research queries submitted
- [ ] 3+ research reports generated
- [ ] Customer self-reported time savings (baseline vs. actual)

**Success Metric**: **80%+ of customers complete first deal within 30 days**

---

#### Day 30: Onboarding Retrospective

**Activities**:

- **Retrospective call** (30 minutes): What worked, what didn't, feedback for product team
- **Satisfaction survey**: 1-5 star rating + NPS question ("Would you recommend?")
- **Next deal planning**: Identify next 2-3 deals for platform use

**Success Metric**: **4/5 stars average onboarding satisfaction**

**Failure Protocol**: If satisfaction <4/5 stars, escalate to Head of Customer Success for recovery plan

---

### Stage 2: Value Realization (Days 31-90)

**Objective**: Customer achieves **30%+ time savings** and confirms ROI within first 90 days

#### Ongoing Engagement (Weeks 5-12)

**Activities**:

- **Bi-weekly check-in calls** (30 minutes): Usage review, deal planning, feature training
- **Usage analytics**: CSM monitors logins, queries, reports (flag if usage drops)
- **Value tracking**: CSM collects time savings data on each deal (baseline vs. actual)
- **Feature adoption**: Introduce advanced features (bulk research, custom templates, collaboration)

**Deliverables**:

- [ ] 2-3 deals completed using platform
- [ ] Quantified time savings per deal (hours saved, cost savings)
- [ ] At least 1 "power user" who fully adopts platform (champion advocate)

---

#### Day 90: Value Realization Review

**Activities**:

- **QBR (Quarterly Business Review)** (60 minutes): Present usage data, time savings, ROI analysis
- **ROI calculation**: Show total cost savings vs. subscription cost (prove positive ROI)
- **Satisfaction survey**: NPS question + feedback on product roadmap
- **Expansion discussion**: Introduce advanced features, additional users, white-glove services

**Success Metric**: **80%+ of customers confirm 30%+ time savings** by Day 90

**Example ROI Presentation**:

> **Q1 2027 Value Realization Summary**<br/>
> **Deals Analyzed**: 3 deals<br/>
> **Time Savings**: 15 hours per deal Ã— 3 deals = 45 hours saved<br/>
> **Cost Savings**: 45 hours Ã— $150/hour = $6,750 saved<br/>
> **Subscription Cost**: $10,000/month Ã— 3 months = $30,000<br/>
> **ROI**: ($6,750 Ã— 4 quarters = $27K annual savings) / $120K annual cost = **23% ROI** (Year 1)<br/>
> **Note**: ROI improves in Year 2+ as team efficiency increases and more deals are analyzed

**Expansion Opportunity**: "You're currently using our Professional plan ($12K/deal). If you upgrade to Enterprise ($10K/deal with volume discount), you'd save $6K annually on 10 deals."

---

### Stage 3: Renewal Preparation (Days 270-365)

**Objective**: Secure renewal 60-90 days before contract expiration

#### Day 270 (90 Days Before Renewal): Renewal Kickoff

**Activities**:

- **Renewal planning call** (30 minutes): Review contract end date, discuss renewal intent
- **Health check**: Review customer health score (green/yellow/red)
- **Usage review**: Are they actively using platform? (If not, why not?)
- **Satisfaction check**: Recent NPS score, any unresolved issues?

**Red Flags (Churn Risk Indicators)**:

- ðŸ”´ **Low usage**: <5 research queries/month in last 60 days
- ðŸ”´ **Low satisfaction**: NPS <20 or recent support escalations
- ðŸ”´ **Champion departure**: Key advocate (Managing Director) left firm
- ðŸ”´ **Budget pressure**: Firm reducing technology spend or consolidating vendors

**Action**: If 2+ red flags, escalate to Head of Customer Success + VP Sales for retention plan

---

#### Day 300-330 (60-30 Days Before Renewal): Renewal Negotiation

**Activities**:

- **Renewal proposal**: Present contract renewal terms (pricing, contract length, features)
- **ROI refresher**: Update ROI calculation with full year of data
- **Expansion discussion**: Upsell opportunities (additional users, advanced features, multi-year)
- **Reference request**: Ask happy customers to be references for new prospects (incentive: 5% discount)

**Pricing Strategy**:

- **Base renewal**: Same pricing as Year 1 (no price increase)
- **Volume discount**: If customer did more deals than expected, offer lower price/deal
- **Multi-year discount**: 10% discount for 2-year renewal, 15% for 3-year
- **Expansion pricing**: Advanced features (analytics module) +$20K/year

**Success Metric**: **90%+ renewal intent confirmed** by Day 330 (30 days before expiration)

---

#### Day 330-365 (30 Days Before to Expiration): Contract Execution

**Activities**:

- **Contract signature**: Execute renewal contract (DocuSign or wet signature)
- **Auto-renewal setup**: Set up auto-renewal for future years (opt-in, not opt-out)
- **Thank you & next steps**: Celebrate renewal, plan Year 2 objectives

**Failure Protocol**: If customer does not renew:

1. **Exit interview**: Understand why they churned (price, value, competition, other)
2. **Win-back campaign**: 90 days post-churn, offer 25% discount to win them back
3. **Product feedback**: Share churn reasons with product team (feature gaps, UX issues)

**Success Metric**: **90%+ renewal rate** (measured annually)

## Customer Health Scoring System

### Health Score Formula

**Customer Health Score** (0-100 scale):

| Dimension | Weight | Measurement | Green (80-100) | Yellow (60-79) | Red (<60) |
|-----------|--------|-------------|----------------|----------------|-----------|
| **Product Usage** | 40% | Research queries/month | 20+ queries | 10-19 queries | <10 queries |
| **Engagement** | 20% | Logins/month | 15+ logins | 8-14 logins | <8 logins |
| **Satisfaction** | 20% | NPS (rolling 90 days) | 50+ | 30-49 | <30 |
| **ROI Realization** | 10% | Self-reported time savings | 40%+ | 20-39% | <20% |
| **Support Health** | 10% | Critical tickets (last 60 days) | 0-1 tickets | 2-3 tickets | 4+ tickets |

**Example Calculation**:

| Dimension | Actual | Score (0-100) | Weight | Weighted Score |
|-----------|--------|---------------|--------|----------------|
| **Usage** | 25 queries/mo | 100 | 40% | 40 |
| **Engagement** | 12 logins/mo | 75 | 20% | 15 |
| **Satisfaction** | NPS 45 | 75 | 20% | 15 |
| **ROI** | 35% time savings | 75 | 10% | 7.5 |
| **Support** | 1 critical ticket | 90 | 10% | 9 |
| **TOTAL HEALTH SCORE** | - | - | - | **86.5 (Green)** |

---

### Health Score Actions

**Green (80-100)**: Healthy customer, focus on expansion

- **CSM action**: Continue regular QBRs, introduce advanced features
- **Sales action**: Request referral or case study participation
- **Renewal probability**: 95%+

**Yellow (60-79)**: At-risk customer, needs intervention

- **CSM action**: Increase touch frequency (weekly calls), identify usage barriers
- **Head of CS action**: Join next QBR, create recovery plan
- **Renewal probability**: 70-85%

**Red (<60)**: Severe churn risk, escalate immediately

- **CSM action**: Daily monitoring, hands-on support
- **Executive action**: CEO/CTO/VP Sales call with customer executive (Managing Director)
- **Offering**: Discount (10-20%), free white-glove service, product roadmap commitment
- **Renewal probability**: 40-60%

**Success Metric**: **80%+ customers in green zone** at any given time

## Expansion Revenue Strategy (10-15% Annual Growth)

### Expansion Levers

**Lever 1: Volume Discounts (More Deals)**

- **Current**: Customer doing 10 deals/year at $12K/deal = $120K ARR
- **Expansion**: Customer grows to 15 deals/year at $11K/deal (volume discount) = $165K ARR (+38%)
- **Trigger**: Customer exceeds contracted deal volume by 20%+

**Lever 2: Additional Users**

- **Current**: 3 users (1 Managing Director, 2 analysts)
- **Expansion**: 6 users (add junior associates, invite clients to collaborate)
- **Pricing**: $5K/year per additional user (beyond 5-user base)
- **Revenue**: +$15K ARR for 3 additional users

**Lever 3: Advanced Analytics Module**

- **Features**: Deal comparison dashboards, predictive risk scoring, industry benchmarks
- **Pricing**: +$20K-$30K/year (add-on module)
- **Target**: 50% of customers adopt by Year 2

**Lever 4: White-Glove Research Service**

- **Service**: Dedicated research analyst supports customer on complex deals
- **Pricing**: $5K per deal (on top of $12K platform fee) = $17K total per deal
- **Target**: 20% of customers purchase on 20-30% of their deals

**Lever 5: Multi-Year Contracts (Upfront Revenue)**

- **Current**: 1-year contract, $120K ARR
- **Expansion**: 3-year contract, $120K Ã— 3 = $360K total contract value
- **Discount**: 15% discount for 3-year prepayment = $306K upfront

## Proactive Churn Prevention Tactics

### Tactic 1: Early Warning System (60-90 Days Before Renewal)

**Automated Alerts** (CSM dashboard):

- ðŸ”´ **Usage drop**: 50%+ decline in queries/month (vs. 3-month average)
- ðŸ”´ **Login gap**: No logins for 14+ consecutive days
- ðŸ”´ **NPS decline**: Recent survey response <30 (vs. previous 50+)
- ðŸ”´ **Support spike**: 3+ critical tickets in 30 days

**CSM Response** (within 24 hours of alert):

- Call customer to understand issue
- Create action plan to address root cause
- Escalate to Head of CS if needed

---

### Tactic 2: Executive Business Reviews (EBRs)

**Frequency**: Quarterly for all customers, monthly for at-risk customers

**Participants**:

- **Customer side**: Managing Director or Partner (executive level)
- **Our side**: CEO or VP Sales (peer-to-peer relationship)

**Agenda** (60 minutes):

1. **Business context** (10 min): Customer's M&A market trends, deal pipeline
2. **Value delivered** (20 min): ROI metrics, time savings, success stories
3. **Product roadmap** (15 min): Upcoming features aligned to customer needs
4. **Strategic discussion** (15 min): How to deepen partnership, expansion opportunities

**Outcome**: Strengthen executive relationship, surface strategic issues before they become churn risks

---

### Tactic 3: Customer Advisory Board (Top 10% Customers)

**Membership**: 3-5 customers (top 10% by ARR or strategic value)

**Cadence**: Quarterly meetings (in-person or virtual)

**Benefits for Customers**:

- Early access to new features (beta testing)
- Influence product roadmap (your voice matters)
- Networking with peer M&A firms

**Benefits for Us**:

- Product feedback from most engaged customers
- Advocacy and references (board members = champions)
- Retention insurance (invested in our success)

**Compensation**: $5K annual discount or free advanced features

---

### Tactic 4: Win-Back Campaign (Post-Churn)

**Timing**: 90 days after churn (give customer time to evaluate alternatives)

**Outreach**:

- **Email from CEO**: "We'd love to win you back. Here's what we've improved."
- **Offer**: 25% discount for 6 months (prove value again at lower risk)
- **Product updates**: Highlight features shipped since they left

**Success Rate**: 10-20% of churned customers return (industry benchmark)
