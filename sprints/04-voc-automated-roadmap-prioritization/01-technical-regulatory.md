# Sprint 04, Task 01: Technical & Regulatory Landscape
## VoC Automated Roadmap Prioritization

### Objective

Research technical requirements, regulatory standards, and compliance pathways for: AI-powered Voice of Customer synthesis for automated product roadmap prioritization - transform manual VoC analysis into evidence-based priority recommendations

### Research Questions

1. What are the relevant technical standards and regulations for AI-powered customer data analysis?
2. What are current compliance requirements for processing customer feedback data (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)?
3. What NLP and sentiment analysis technologies are most effective for VoC synthesis?
4. What API integration standards exist for product management tools (Productboard, Jira, etc.)?
5. What technical risks or challenges exist in automated feedback aggregation and analysis?
6. What data privacy and security requirements must be met for handling customer data?
7. What precedents exist for AI-powered product management tools receiving regulatory approval?
8. What verification and validation approaches ensure accuracy of AI-generated prioritization recommendations?

### Expected Deliverables

- Technical standards analysis for AI/ML in customer data processing (2-3 files)
- Data privacy and compliance requirements (GDPR, CCPA, SOC 2) (2-3 files)
- NLP and sentiment analysis technology landscape (2-3 files)
- API integration standards and best practices (1-2 files)
- Risk assessment for automated VoC analysis (1-2 files)
- Synthesis document with key findings (1 file)

### Success Criteria

- Minimum 6 research files created
- All claims cited with authoritative sources
- Technical accuracy verified
- Regulatory pathways clearly documented
- Data privacy requirements comprehensively covered
