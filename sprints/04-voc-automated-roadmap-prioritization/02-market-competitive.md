# Sprint 04, Task 02: Market & Competitive Assessment
## VoC Automated Roadmap Prioritization

### Objective

Analyze market opportunity, size estimates, competitive landscape, and customer needs for: AI-powered Voice of Customer synthesis for automated product roadmap prioritization - transform manual VoC analysis into evidence-based priority recommendations

### Research Questions

1. What is the total addressable market (TAM) for product management software and VoC tools?
2. What is serviceable addressable market (SAM) and serviceable obtainable market (SOM) for automated VoC prioritization?
3. Who are the key competitors (Productboard, ProdPad, Aha!, UserVoice, Canny) and what are their offerings?
4. What are product managers' pain points with current roadmap prioritization approaches?
5. What unmet needs exist in the VoC analysis and synthesis market?
6. What is the market growth rate (CAGR) for PM tools and AI automation in product management?
7. What pricing models and revenue potential exist for VoC automation platforms?
8. What is the competitive differentiation opportunity (segment analysis, churn prediction, evidence-based prioritization)?

### Expected Deliverables

- Market sizing analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM) with sources (1-2 files)
- Competitive landscape mapping (Productboard, ProdPad, Aha!, UserVoice, Canny) (2-3 files)
- Customer needs assessment (PM pain points, prioritization challenges) (1-2 files)
- Pricing and business model analysis (1-2 files)
- Growth drivers and market trends (AI adoption, data-driven culture) (1 file)
- Synthesis document with market opportunity summary (1 file)

### Success Criteria

- Market sizes quantified with sources
- Competitors identified and analyzed
- Customer insights documented with evidence
- Business case outlined with ROI projections
- Competitive differentiation clearly articulated
