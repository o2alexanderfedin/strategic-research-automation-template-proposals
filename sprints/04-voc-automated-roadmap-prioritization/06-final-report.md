# Sprint 04, Task 06: Final Report Synthesis
## VoC Automated Roadmap Prioritization

### Objective

Synthesize all research findings into comprehensive strategic report with scoring and go/no-go recommendation for: AI-powered Voice of Customer synthesis for automated product roadmap prioritization - transform manual VoC analysis into evidence-based priority recommendations

### Process

This task is executed by the report-synthesizer skill, which:

1. Reads all research files from Tasks 01-05
2. Applies scoring rubric from config/scoring-rubric.yml
3. Generates comprehensive strategic report (5,000-7,500 words)
4. Calculates weighted opportunity score (0-100)
5. Provides clear go/no-go recommendation

### Report Structure

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Opportunity overview
   - Key findings
   - Opportunity score and recommendation
   - Investment required and expected returns

2. **Market Opportunity Analysis**
   - TAM/SAM/SOM sizing
   - Market growth drivers
   - Customer segments and pain points
   - Competitive landscape

3. **Technical Approach & Architecture**
   - System architecture and components
   - Technology stack and integrations
   - Data privacy and security
   - Scalability considerations

4. **Certification & Compliance**
   - Compliance requirements (SOC 2, GDPR, CCPA)
   - Testing strategy
   - Timeline and costs

5. **Implementation Strategy**
   - Phased roadmap (MVP → Pilot → Launch → Scale)
   - Resource requirements
   - Partnership strategy
   - Customer acquisition plan

6. **Risk Assessment**
   - Technical risks (integration complexity, data quality)
   - Market risks (competitive response, slow adoption)
   - Mitigation strategies

7. **Opportunity Scoring** (5 dimensions)
   - Market Opportunity (size, growth, urgency)
   - Technical Feasibility (complexity, risk, timeline)
   - Competitive Advantage (differentiation, barriers)
   - Financial Viability (ROI, payback period)
   - Strategic Fit (capabilities, vision alignment)

8. **Go/No-Go Recommendation**
   - Clear recommendation with justification
   - Key success factors
   - Next steps

9. **Appendices**
   - Detailed research citations
   - Additional analysis

### Success Criteria

- Report is 5,000-7,500 words
- All required sections present
- Scoring methodology applied correctly
- Clear recommendation with rationale
- Publication-ready quality
- Proper formatting with tables, lists, and Mermaid diagrams
