# Sprint 03, Task 02: Market & Competitive Assessment
## Corporate R&D Portfolio Intelligence

### Objective
Analyze market opportunity, size estimates, competitive landscape, and customer needs for: AI-powered portfolio intelligence for R&D resource optimization - systematically evaluate strategic fit of R&D initiatives

### Research Questions
1. What is the total addressable market (TAM) for R&D portfolio management software?
2. What is serviceable addressable market (SAM) and serviceable obtainable market (SOM)?
3. Who are the key competitors (McKinsey, BCG, Planview, Sopheon) and what are their offerings?
4. What are customer pain points (zombie projects, resource waste, portfolio misalignment)?
5. What is the market growth rate (CAGR) for innovation management and R&D optimization?
6. What pricing models and revenue potential exist (per-project, portfolio review, annual retainer)?

### Expected Deliverables
- Market sizing analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM for R&D portfolio management)
- Competitive landscape mapping (consultants vs. software vs. internal teams)
- Customer needs assessment (CTOs, VPs of R&D, portfolio managers)
- Pricing and business model analysis
- Growth drivers analysis (R&D budget trends, digital transformation)
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Market sizes quantified with sources ($8-12B TAM estimated)
- Competitors identified and analyzed (consulting firms, software vendors)
- Customer insights documented (Fortune 500-1000 R&D leaders)
- Business case outlined with ROI projections
