# Sprint 03, Task 01: Technical & Regulatory Landscape
## Corporate R&D Portfolio Intelligence

### Objective
Research technical requirements, regulatory standards, and compliance pathways for: AI-powered portfolio intelligence for R&D resource optimization - systematically evaluate strategic fit of R&D initiatives

### Research Questions
1. What are the relevant technical standards and regulations for R&D portfolio management software?
2. What are current compliance requirements (data privacy, security, audit trails)?
3. What verification and validation approaches exist for portfolio decision support systems?
4. What precedents or prior approvals are relevant in R&D management software?
5. What technical risks or challenges exist in automated portfolio intelligence?
6. What documentation and testing is required for enterprise R&D software?

### Expected Deliverables
- Technical standards analysis (5-7 files)
- Regulatory pathway documentation
- Compliance requirements summary (GDPR, SOC2, ISO 27001)
- Risk assessment for AI-driven portfolio recommendations
- Data security and privacy requirements
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Minimum 5 research files created
- All claims cited with authoritative sources
- Technical accuracy verified
- Regulatory pathways clearly documented
- Security and compliance requirements specified
