# Sprint 03, Task 06: Final Report Synthesis
## Corporate R&D Portfolio Intelligence

### Objective
Synthesize all research findings into comprehensive strategic report with scoring and go/no-go recommendation for: AI-powered portfolio intelligence for R&D resource optimization - systematically evaluate strategic fit of R&D initiatives

### Process
This task is executed by the report-synthesizer skill, which:
1. Reads all research files from Tasks 01-05
2. Applies scoring rubric from config/scoring-rubric.yml
3. Generates 10-page strategic report (5,000-7,500 words)
4. Calculates weighted opportunity score (0-100)
5. Provides clear go/no-go recommendation

### Report Structure
1. **Executive Summary** (1-2 pages)
   - Opportunity overview
   - Market size and customer needs
   - Key findings and recommendation

2. **Market Opportunity Analysis**
   - TAM/SAM/SOM for R&D portfolio management ($8-12B TAM)
   - Customer segments (Fortune 500-1000 CTOs, VPs of R&D)
   - Pain points (zombie projects, portfolio misalignment, resource waste)
   - Growth drivers (R&D budget growth, digital transformation)

3. **Technical Approach & Architecture**
   - System architecture and components
   - Technology stack and data sources
   - Integration with enterprise R&D tools
   - Security and compliance requirements

4. **Competitive Landscape**
   - Consulting firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain)
   - Software vendors (Planview, Sopheon, IdeaScale)
   - Differentiation strategy (speed, cost, comprehensiveness)

5. **Certification & Timeline**
   - Enterprise compliance requirements (SOC2, ISO 27001)
   - Validation and testing strategy
   - Certification timeline and costs

6. **Implementation Strategy**
   - Phased roadmap (Pilot → Sales Launch → Scale)
   - Resource requirements and investment
   - Partnership strategy
   - Quick wins and long-term objectives

7. **Risk Assessment**
   - Key risks (internal politics, data access, long sales cycles)
   - Mitigation strategies (executive sponsorship, pilot programs)
   - Success factors

8. **Opportunity Scoring** (5 dimensions)
   - Market Opportunity (size, growth, accessibility)
   - Technical Feasibility (complexity, time to MVP)
   - Competitive Position (differentiation, barriers to entry)
   - Financial Viability (pricing, margins, payback period)
   - Strategic Fit (alignment, capabilities, scalability)

9. **Go/No-Go Recommendation**
   - Overall assessment
   - Key decision factors
   - Recommended next steps
   - Conditions for success

10. **Appendices**
    - Research file references
    - Data sources and citations
    - Methodology notes

### Success Criteria
- Report is 5,000-7,500 words
- All required sections present and comprehensive
- Scoring methodology applied with evidence
- Clear recommendation with rationale
- Publication-ready quality for executive review
- Dual-audience (business executives and technical decision-makers)
