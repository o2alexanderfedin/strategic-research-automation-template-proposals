# Sprint 03, Task 04: Certification & Testing Strategy
## Corporate R&D Portfolio Intelligence

### Objective
Define certification pathway, testing requirements, and quality assurance approach for: AI-powered portfolio intelligence for R&D resource optimization - systematically evaluate strategic fit of R&D initiatives

### Research Questions
1. What certifications or approvals are required for enterprise R&D software (SOC2, ISO 27001)?
2. What testing standards must be met (accuracy of market sizing, portfolio scoring)?
3. What is the certification process and timeline for enterprise security compliance?
4. What documentation is required for approval by enterprise IT and security teams?
5. What testing infrastructure and capabilities are needed (pilot programs, validation)?
6. What are costs and risks in certification process?

### Expected Deliverables
- Certification pathway roadmap (SOC2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR compliance)
- Testing strategy (portfolio recommendation accuracy, data security penetration testing)
- Validation methodology (pilot customer validation, ROI verification)
- Documentation requirements (security audit trails, methodology transparency)
- Timeline and milestone analysis (6-12 month certification timeline)
- Cost estimates for compliance and certification
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Certification path clearly defined with standards
- Testing requirements specified (security, accuracy, performance)
- Timeline realistic and achievable (aligned with sales cycle)
- Costs estimated for compliance infrastructure
- Risk mitigation strategies for enterprise adoption barriers
