# Sprint 06, Task 02: Market & Competitive Assessment
## Digital Transformation Consulting Research Enablement

### Objective
Analyze market opportunity, size estimates, competitive landscape, and customer needs for: Continuous Intelligence Platform for Digital Transformation Advisory - enable DX consultants with real-time technology intelligence, implementation benchmarking, and stakeholder-specific business cases

### Research Questions
1. What is the total addressable market (TAM) for digital transformation consulting intelligence and research tools?
2. What is serviceable addressable market (SAM) and serviceable obtainable market (SOM) focusing on Big 3/Big 4 and boutique DX advisory firms?
3. Who are the key competitors and what are their offerings (Gartner, Forrester, AlphaSense, CB Insights, internal research teams)?
4. What are customer pain points and unmet needs for DX consultants (technology overload, benchmarking gaps, stakeholder alignment, retainer-model enablement)?
5. What is the market growth rate (CAGR) for DX consulting and adjacent intelligence markets?
6. What pricing models and revenue potential exist (per-consultant licensing, practice subscriptions, implementation services)?

### Expected Deliverables
- Market sizing analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM with 2024-2032 projections)
- Competitive landscape mapping (Gartner/Forrester vs. document search vs. manual research)
- Customer needs assessment (DX Practice Leaders, Partners)
- Pricing and business model analysis ($5K-10K per consultant, practice-wide deployments)
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Market sizes quantified with sources
- Competitors identified and analyzed with whitespace opportunities
- Customer insights documented with buyer personas
- Business case outlined with revenue projections
