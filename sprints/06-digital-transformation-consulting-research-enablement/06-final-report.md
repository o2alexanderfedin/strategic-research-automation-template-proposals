# Sprint 06, Task 06: Final Report Synthesis
## Digital Transformation Consulting Research Enablement

### Objective
Synthesize all research findings into comprehensive strategic report with scoring and go/no-go recommendation for: Continuous Intelligence Platform for Digital Transformation Advisory - enable DX consultants with real-time technology intelligence, implementation benchmarking, and stakeholder-specific business cases

### Process
This task is executed by the report-synthesizer skill, which:
1. Reads all research files from Tasks 01-05
2. Applies scoring rubric from config/scoring-rubric.yml
3. Generates 10-page strategic report (5,000-7,500 words)
4. Calculates weighted opportunity score (0-100)
5. Provides clear go/no-go recommendation

### Report Structure
1. **Executive Summary**: DX consulting intelligence platform opportunity overview
2. **Market Opportunity Analysis**: TAM $1.4B-$3.2B, 13.4% CAGR, 2,000-3,000 DX consultants addressable
3. **Technical Approach & Architecture**: Technology monitoring engine, benchmarking database, business case generation, CRM integration
4. **Certification & Timeline**: SOC 2/ISO 27001 requirements, MVP 4-6 months, enterprise-ready 8-12 months
5. **Implementation Strategy**: 5-phase roadmap (Product Dev → Pilot → Case Studies → Sales → Scale)
6. **Risk Assessment**: Content accuracy, technology complexity, sales cycle, competitive response mitigation
7. **Opportunity Scoring**: Market size, growth rate, competitive differentiation, technical feasibility, strategic fit
8. **Go/No-Go Recommendation**: Based on highest growth potential (13.4% CAGR) and strategic positioning for retainer-model consulting
9. **Appendices**: Detailed market data, competitive analysis, technology stack, pricing models

### Success Criteria
- Report is 5,000-7,500 words
- All required sections present
- Scoring methodology applied across 5 dimensions
- Clear recommendation with rationale (context: should be pursued after Research Operations Optimization to build consulting firm credibility)
- Publication-ready quality suitable for stakeholder presentation
