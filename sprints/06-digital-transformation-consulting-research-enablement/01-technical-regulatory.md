# Sprint 06, Task 01: Technical & Regulatory Landscape
## Digital Transformation Consulting Research Enablement

### Objective
Research technical requirements, regulatory standards, and compliance pathways for: Continuous Intelligence Platform for Digital Transformation Advisory - enable DX consultants with real-time technology intelligence, implementation benchmarking, and stakeholder-specific business cases

### Research Questions
1. What are the relevant technical standards and regulations for consulting intelligence platforms?
2. What are current compliance requirements for data aggregation, AI-powered research, and business intelligence tools?
3. What verification and validation approaches exist for technology landscape monitoring and benchmarking accuracy?
4. What precedents or prior approvals are relevant (e.g., existing analyst platforms, business intelligence tools)?
5. What technical risks or challenges exist in real-time technology tracking, case study aggregation, and multi-stakeholder business case generation?
6. What documentation and testing is required for enterprise SaaS platforms serving consulting firms?

### Expected Deliverables
- Technical standards analysis (5-7 files)
- Regulatory pathway documentation
- Compliance requirements summary (data privacy, AI ethics, intellectual property)
- Risk assessment (content accuracy, technology complexity, integration challenges)
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Minimum 5 research files created
- All claims cited with authoritative sources
- Technical accuracy verified
- Regulatory pathways clearly documented (GDPR, SOC 2, consulting industry standards)
