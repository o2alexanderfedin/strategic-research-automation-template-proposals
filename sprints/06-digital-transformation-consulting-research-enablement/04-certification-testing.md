# Sprint 06, Task 04: Certification & Testing Strategy
## Digital Transformation Consulting Research Enablement

### Objective
Define certification pathway, testing requirements, and quality assurance approach for: Continuous Intelligence Platform for Digital Transformation Advisory - enable DX consultants with real-time technology intelligence, implementation benchmarking, and stakeholder-specific business cases

### Research Questions
1. What certifications or approvals are required for enterprise SaaS platforms serving consulting firms (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR compliance)?
2. What testing standards must be met for content accuracy, technology tracking reliability, and business case validity?
3. What is the certification process and timeline for enterprise security compliance?
4. What documentation is required for approval by Big 3/Big 4 consulting firm procurement and IT security teams?
5. What testing infrastructure and capabilities are needed (expert review network, multi-source validation, confidence scoring)?
6. What are costs and risks in certification process (security audits, content validation, enterprise readiness)?

### Expected Deliverables
- Certification pathway roadmap (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR compliance timelines)
- Testing strategy and test plans (content accuracy validation, technology monitoring reliability, business case validity testing)
- Documentation requirements (security controls, data privacy policies, content validation methodology)
- Timeline and milestone analysis (MVP to enterprise-ready: 4-12 months)
- Cost estimates (security audits, expert review panel, certification fees)
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Certification path clearly defined for enterprise consulting firm deployment
- Testing requirements specified (content accuracy thresholds, technology tracking coverage, business case validation)
- Timeline realistic and achievable (phased approach from pilot to enterprise)
- Costs estimated with contingency
