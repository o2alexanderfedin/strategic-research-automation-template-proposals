# Sprint 05, Task 06: Final Report Synthesis
## M&A Due Diligence Research Acceleration

### Objective

Synthesize all research from Tasks 01-05 into comprehensive strategic report with opportunity scoring and go/no-go recommendation

### Input Sources

- Task 01: Technical & Regulatory Landscape (temp/05-ma-due-diligence-acceleration/01-technical/)
- Task 02: Market & Competitive Assessment (temp/05-ma-due-diligence-acceleration/02-market/)
- Task 03: Solution Architecture Design (temp/05-ma-due-diligence-acceleration/03-architecture/)
- Task 04: Certification & Testing Strategy (temp/05-ma-due-diligence-acceleration/04-compliance/)
- Task 05: Implementation Roadmap (temp/05-ma-due-diligence-acceleration/05-roadmap/)

### Report Structure

1. **Executive Summary** (1-2 pages)
   - Opportunity overview
   - Market size and growth
   - Competitive advantage
   - Investment required
   - Opportunity score
   - Go/no-go recommendation

2. **Market Opportunity** (3-5 pages)
   - TAM/SAM/SOM analysis
   - Customer pain points and value proposition
   - Competitive landscape
   - Market trends and growth drivers

3. **Technical Feasibility** (3-5 pages)
   - Core capabilities and technology stack
   - Implementation complexity
   - Integration requirements
   - Security and compliance pathways

4. **Solution Architecture** (3-5 pages)
   - High-level system design
   - Key architectural decisions
   - POC specifications
   - Scalability considerations

5. **Certification & Compliance** (2-3 pages)
   - Required certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
   - Testing and validation methodologies
   - Timeline and budget estimates

6. **Implementation Roadmap** (3-5 pages)
   - Phased rollout plan
   - Partnership strategy
   - Go-to-market approach
   - Resource requirements
   - Success metrics

7. **Opportunity Scoring** (1-2 pages)
   - Scoring breakdown by category (using config/scoring-rubric.yml)
   - Overall score (0-100)
   - Strengths and weaknesses
   - Risk assessment

8. **Recommendation** (1 page)
   - Go/no-go decision
   - Rationale
   - Critical success factors
   - Next steps

### Success Criteria

- Report length: 5,000-7,500 words
- All findings from Tasks 01-05 synthesized
- Opportunity score calculated using scoring rubric
- Clear go/no-go recommendation with justification
- Executive summary suitable for stakeholder presentation
- All claims supported by research evidence
- Professional formatting for PDF export
