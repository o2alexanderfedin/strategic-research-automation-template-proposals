# Sprint 05, Task 02: Market & Competitive Assessment
## M&A Due Diligence Research Acceleration

### Objective

Analyze market size (TAM/SAM/SOM), competitive landscape, customer needs, and pricing dynamics for: AI-Powered Due Diligence Research Platform targeting investment banks and M&A advisory firms (50-200 FTE)

### Research Questions

1. What is the Total Addressable Market (TAM) for M&A due diligence research automation?
2. What is the Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) for mid-size investment banks and M&A advisory firms?
3. What is the Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) for Year 1-3?
4. Who are the primary competitors in due diligence automation (tools, consultants, research firms)?
5. What are current customer workflows and pain points in M&A due diligence?
6. What is the competitive pricing landscape ($100K-150K subscription + $15K-25K per deal)?
7. What customer segments have the highest willingness to pay?
8. What are the key buying criteria for investment banks and M&A advisory firms?
9. What is the analyst burnout crisis impact on demand (30-50% annual turnover)?
10. What M&A market trends drive demand (deal volume, AI adoption in PE/VC, time compression)?

### Expected Deliverables

- TAM/SAM/SOM analysis with market sizing calculations (1-2 files)
- Competitive landscape analysis (external consultants, vertical DD firms, document review AI) (2-3 files)
- Customer segmentation and persona profiles (Managing Directors M&A, Partners) (1-2 files)
- Pricing strategy and willingness-to-pay analysis (1 file)
- Analyst burnout crisis research and demand drivers (1-2 files)
- M&A market trends and growth drivers (deal volume, AI adoption) (1-2 files)
- Synthesis document with key findings (1 file)

### Success Criteria

- Minimum 5 research files created
- Market sizing validated with multiple authoritative sources
- Competitive analysis includes feature/price comparisons
- Customer pain points quantified with evidence
- Pricing strategy supported by market data
- Growth drivers clearly identified and substantiated
