# Sprint 02, Task 06: Final Report Synthesis
## Venture Capital Competitive Intelligence

### Objective
Synthesize all research findings into comprehensive strategic report with scoring and go/no-go recommendation for: AI-powered competitive intelligence automation for VC investment decisions

### Process
This task is executed by the report-synthesizer skill, which:
1. Reads all research files from Tasks 01-05
2. Applies scoring rubric from config/scoring-rubric.yml
3. Generates 10-page strategic report (5,000-7,500 words)
4. Calculates weighted opportunity score (0-100)
5. Provides clear go/no-go recommendation

### Report Structure
1. **Executive Summary**
   - Opportunity overview
   - Key findings snapshot
   - Go/no-go recommendation with rationale
   - Investment required and expected returns

2. **Market Opportunity Analysis**
   - TAM/SAM/SOM quantification
   - Market growth drivers and trends
   - Competitive landscape assessment
   - Customer pain points and willingness to pay

3. **Technical Approach & Architecture**
   - Solution architecture overview
   - Key technology components
   - Integration strategy
   - Technical feasibility assessment

4. **Certification & Timeline**
   - Regulatory compliance pathway
   - Certification requirements (SOC 2, GDPR)
   - Testing and quality assurance strategy
   - Timeline to market readiness

5. **Implementation Strategy**
   - Phased roadmap (MVP → Pilot → Scale)
   - Resource requirements and team structure
   - Go-to-market strategy
   - Customer acquisition approach

6. **Risk Assessment**
   - Technical risks (data quality, AI accuracy)
   - Market risks (competition, adoption)
   - Execution risks (team, timeline, budget)
   - Mitigation strategies for critical risks

7. **Opportunity Scoring (5 dimensions)**
   - Market Attractiveness (25%)
   - Technical Feasibility (25%)
   - Competitive Position (20%)
   - Execution Readiness (15%)
   - Strategic Fit (15%)

8. **Go/No-Go Recommendation**
   - Weighted score analysis
   - Key decision factors
   - Recommended next steps
   - Investment thesis summary

9. **Appendices**
   - Detailed market sizing calculations
   - Competitive feature matrix
   - Architecture diagrams
   - Implementation Gantt chart

### Success Criteria
- Report is 5,000-7,500 words
- All required sections present and comprehensive
- Scoring methodology applied consistently
- Clear recommendation with evidence-based rationale
- Publication-ready quality (professional formatting, cited sources)
