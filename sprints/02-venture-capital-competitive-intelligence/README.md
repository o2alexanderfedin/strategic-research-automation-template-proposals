# Sprint 02: Venture Capital Competitive Intelligence

**Description**: AI-powered competitive intelligence automation for VC investment decisions<br/>
**Status**: Defined (not yet executed)<br/>
**Tasks**: 6 (Technical, Market, Architecture, Certification, Roadmap, Report)

## Overview

This sprint investigates the strategic opportunity: **Venture Capital Competitive Intelligence**

Automate competitive landscape analysis for venture capital firms evaluating 200+ company pitches annually, reducing research time from 2-3 hours to 15-30 minutes per company while providing comprehensive competitive intelligence briefs.

## Tasks

1. **Task 01**: Technical & Regulatory Landscape
   - AI compliance for financial services (SEC, FINRA, GDPR)
   - Data quality and verification standards
   - Technical risk assessment (hallucination, bias, privacy)

2. **Task 02**: Market & Competitive Assessment
   - TAM/SAM/SOM for VC competitive intelligence tools
   - Competitive analysis (PitchBook, Crunchbase, CB Insights, AlphaSense)
   - Customer pain points and pricing research

3. **Task 03**: Solution Architecture Design
   - System architecture (data aggregation, AI synthesis, report generation)
   - Technology stack (LLMs, knowledge graphs, APIs)
   - Integration with VC tools (Affinity CRM, deal flow systems)

4. **Task 04**: Certification & Testing Strategy
   - SOC 2, GDPR compliance pathways
   - Accuracy testing and validation frameworks
   - Security and privacy certifications

5. **Task 05**: Implementation Roadmap
   - Phased rollout (MVP → Pilot → Scale)
   - Resource requirements and timeline
   - Risk mitigation and go-to-market strategy

6. **Task 06**: Final Report Synthesis
   - Comprehensive strategic report (5,000-7,500 words)
   - Opportunity scoring (0-100)
   - Go/no-go recommendation

- 80%+ citation density

- Professional formatting with Mermaid diagrams
