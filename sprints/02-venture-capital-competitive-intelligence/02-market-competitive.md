# Sprint 02, Task 02: Market & Competitive Assessment
## Venture Capital Competitive Intelligence

### Objective
Analyze market opportunity, size estimates, competitive landscape, and customer needs for: AI-powered competitive intelligence automation for VC investment decisions

### Research Questions
1. What is the total addressable market (TAM) for VC competitive intelligence tools?
2. What is serviceable addressable market (SAM) and serviceable obtainable market (SOM)?
3. Who are the key competitors and what are their offerings (PitchBook, Crunchbase, CB Insights, AlphaSense)?
4. What are VC customer pain points and unmet needs in competitive research workflows?
5. What is the market growth rate (CAGR) for VC software and research tools?
6. What pricing models and revenue potential exist (subscription, per-analysis, enterprise)?

### Expected Deliverables
- Market sizing analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM with sources)
- Competitive landscape mapping (feature comparison, positioning)
- Customer needs assessment (VC interviews, surveys, pain point analysis)
- Pricing and business model analysis (benchmarking vs. competitors)
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Market sizes quantified with credible sources (industry reports, VC surveys)
- Competitors identified and analyzed (feature matrix, pricing comparison)
- Customer insights documented (interview quotes, pain point prioritization)
- Business case outlined with revenue projections
