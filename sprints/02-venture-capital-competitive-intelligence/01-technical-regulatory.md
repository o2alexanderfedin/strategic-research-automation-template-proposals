# Sprint 02, Task 01: Technical & Regulatory Landscape
## Venture Capital Competitive Intelligence

### Objective
Research technical requirements, regulatory standards, and compliance pathways for: AI-powered competitive intelligence automation for VC investment decisions

### Research Questions
1. What are the relevant technical standards and regulations for AI-powered financial research tools?
2. What are current compliance requirements for VC firms using AI tools (SEC, FINRA, data privacy)?
3. What verification and validation approaches exist for competitive intelligence accuracy?
4. What precedents or prior approvals are relevant for AI research tools in financial services?
5. What technical risks or challenges exist (data quality, hallucination, bias)?
6. What documentation and testing is required for enterprise adoption by regulated entities?

### Expected Deliverables
- Technical standards analysis (5-7 files)
- Regulatory pathway documentation for AI in financial services
- Compliance requirements summary (SEC, FINRA, GDPR, SOC 2)
- Risk assessment (data accuracy, AI reliability, privacy)
- Synthesis document

### Success Criteria
- Minimum 5 research files created
- All claims cited with authoritative sources (SEC regulations, FINRA guidance, industry standards)
- Technical accuracy verified against current AI compliance frameworks
- Regulatory pathways clearly documented for VC/financial services context
